An event for

Travel & Tourism

The Sri Lanka Association of Inbound Tour Operators (SLAITO) the Apex Body representing the Inbound Tour Operator Community in Sri Lanka, will organize ‘Sancharaka Udawa 2024’ tourism products meet mart on 17th and 18th of May 2024 at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Exhibition Halls at BMICH. This is the 11th edition that this much looked forward to exhibition is being held.

Accommodation

Events/Conference

Sporting

Outbound Travel Agents

Ticketing and holiday agents catering to the local market and offering travel packages around the world.
